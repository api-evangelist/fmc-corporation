ELEVEST® INSECT CONTROL lana.beckard Mon, 05/11/2020 - 18:53 Insecticides & Miticides Elevest ® insect control powered by Rynaxypyr ® active and bifenthrin delivers rapid knockdown and long-lasting residual control to protect row and specialty crops from damaging insect pressure. This convenient premix combines industry-leading control of Lepidopteran pests with broad-spectrum performance against chewing and sucking pests like soybean podworm, western bean cutworm, soybean loopers, stink bugs and more. With flexible at-plant applications, Elevest ® also helps crops get off to a stronger start by protecting against early-season soil pests. Controls more than 40 pests, including soybean podworm, cotton bollworm, cutworms, armyworms, loopers and stink bugs. Features the maximum active ingredient ratio of industry-leading Rynaxypyr active (Group 28) for control of Lepidopteran pests, with the fast knockdown of superior pyrethroid, bifenthrin (Group 3A), for broad-spectrum and better overall control. At-plant soil applications protect seeds and seedlings during critical early growth from cutworms, seedcorn maggots, wireworms and other soil borne pests. Ideal fit for growers fighting the worm complex plus other insects simultaneously. Delivers fast-acting, long-lasting residual insect control of targeted pests. Rainfast when the application is dry. Active ingredients Bifenthrin Rynaxypyr active (chlorantraniliprole) Elevest ® insect control is a fully loaded, convenient premix designed to deliver fast knockdown and long-lasting residual control of Lepidopteran insects and other damaging pests in corn, soybeans, cotton and select specialty crops. Its dual-active formulation combines Rynaxypyr ® for control of Lepidopteran pests with the rapid knockdown of bifenthrin, delivering broad-spectrum control across multiple pests, including cotton bollworm, western bean cutworm, soybean podworm, soybean loopers, stink bugs and more. The optimized ratio of these two premier active ingredients improves performance on stink bugs and plant bugs compared to similar premix options. At-plant soil application flexibility helps protect against early-season in-furrow pests during critical crop establishment, supporting stronger stands and more uniform emergence. From below-ground pests at planting to late-season foliar and pod feeders, Elevest ® delivers broad-spectrum protection to help preserve crop quality and maximize yield potential.
